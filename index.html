I am a PhD candidate in economics at Georgetown University. My research applies structural econometric methods to economic problems in labor markets, education, and sports. I am on the job market in 2026–2027.

My job market paper, "Optimal Dynamic Selection Under Costly Evaluation: Evidence from Graduate Admissions," develops and estimates structural models of an admissions committee's dynamic selection problem under costly evaluation, formally tests whether the committee optimizes, and quantifies deviation gains from adopting optimal decision rules.

I also do sports analytics. My work on NFL quarterback prospect evaluation (with Alexandre Olbrecht and Aaron Schatz) has been featured annually on ESPN since 2021, and my research on professional tennis (with Axel Anderson, John Rust, and Kin-ping Wong) was published in the Journal of Political Economy in 2025. More of my tennis analytics work is at Topspin Shot Research.
